Profile:

SHARTEL GROUP is a creative enterprise, where innovation building designs and interior environment is a way of life. We hold experties in providing start-to-end services from inspiring clients with project feasibility – through conceptualization – to realization of design and actual project delivery in the built and natural form. For over 25 years we’ve led with vision, contributing to many of the world’s most ambitious projects, iconic designs and interior offering remarkable experiences. We also collaborate with the expertise from all over the world, bringing experience from every major market of the globe. With multidisciplinary professionals and staff, we are a highly professional Architectural consulting firm.

  • Showrooms and Shop Interiors
  • Entertainment,Tourism and Educational/Sports complex

  • Residential Interiors
